Our Mission

We are on a mission to further prevent, detect and manage chronic conditions,

in real-time and non-invasively. We want to empower you through technology

to live your best life, and will help you every beat of the way.

Live your best life.

Our wrist-worn Heart Watch wearable has been clinically validated to continuously measure heart rate and heart rate variability (HRV) to objectively quantify and assess stress, cardio-respiratory fitness (via percentage of your max volume of oxygen consumption), cardiovascular performance and irregular heart rhythms.

We have integrated our Heart Watch technology into a remote patient monitoring system, enabling physicians to monitor their patient's stress, weight, blood pressure and heart rhythms between office visits. Through personalized care plans and higher means of communication.

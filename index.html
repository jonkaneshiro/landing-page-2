About us

Founded by a team of experienced medical device, signal processing and electrical engineers, LIVMOR has become an organization aligned with a simple, yet powerful mission: to connect physicians with patients through technology and to empower users to take control of their health. We are motivated to fix the fragmented healthcare system and flawed ideology by implementing integrated technology with a human-centric approach.

We believe that a person's health is more than the diagnosed condition or disease, and is often times a response to an inbalance from multiple stressors. With this in mind, we have developed our system to view the whole human, as opposed to the disease, in order to provide physicians and caregivers the crucial information they need to make sound decisions.

We have dedicated our careers to engineering the most advanced remote patient monitoring system in the world, integrating our proprietary LIVMOR Heart Watch, which analytically quantifies autonomic nervous system responses in real-time and non-invasively.

Remote Patient Monitoring

For patients, providers, physicians and families.

Our remote patient monitoring platform seamlessly integrates and continually transmits cardiorespiratory, arrythmia, weight, blood pressure and blood glucose information through FDA-approved or clinically-validated devices. The heart of LIVMOR's remote patient monitoring system is our flagship Heart Watch wearable, which has been clinically-validated to continuously measure heart rate and heart rate variability, to accurately quantify and assess autonomic stress, recovery, cardiorespiratory fitness (VO2max) and irregular heart rhythms.

Patient information is securely and wirelessly transmitted, in real-time, to the patient's physician, caregiver and/or family. Through a higher means of communication and monitoring, physicians are now able to create more personalized care plans, provide continuity of care, reduce healthcare costs, and most importantly, be there when their patients need them most.

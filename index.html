We are on a mission to further prevent, detect and manage chronic conditions, in real-time and non-invasively. It is our goal to empower you to live your best life, and we will be with you every beat of the way.

Our wrist-worn Heart Watch wearable has been clinically validated to continuously measure heart rate and heart rate variability (HRV) to objectively quantify and assess stress, cardio-respiratory fitness (percentage of max oxygen consumption), cardiovascular performance and irregular heart rhythms.

The Heart Watch is integrated into a comprehensive remote patient monitoring system, enabling physicians to monitor their patient's stress, weight, blood pressure and heart rhythms, in real-time and between office visits. Through personalized care plans and a higher means of communication, we provide the necessary tools to enhance quality of care and ensure peace of mind.
